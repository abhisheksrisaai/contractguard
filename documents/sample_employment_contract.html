EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement is made between ABC Corp Ltd. ("Employer") and the undersigned ("Employee").

  1. Notice Period

    The Employee shall provide 7 days written notice prior to resignation. The Employer may deduct salary for insufficient notice period.

  2. Working Hours

    The Employee shall work from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM, Monday through Saturday. Overtime work shall be compensated at the regular hourly rate. The Employee may be required to work on Sundays as needed by business requirements.

  3. Non-Compete

    The Employee agrees not to engage in any business similar to the Employer's business anywhere in India for a period of 3 years after termination of employment. No compensation shall be paid during the restricted period.

  4. Confidentiality

    The Employee shall maintain confidentiality of all company information for a period of 5 years after termination. This includes all information learned during employment regardless of whether it is publicly available.

  5. Intellectual Property

    All inventions, discoveries, and works created by the Employee during employment shall be the exclusive property of the Employer, regardless of whether created during working hours or using company resources.

  6. Salary

    Salary shall be paid by the 15th of each month. The Employer reserves the right to deduct amounts for any reason deemed appropriate.

  7. Termination

    The Employer may terminate employment at any time without notice or cause. The Employee forfeits all unpaid benefits upon termination.

  8. Indemnity

    The Employee shall indemnify the Employer against all claims, losses, and expenses arising from the Employee's performance of duties, regardless of fault.

  9. Transfer

    The Employee agrees to transfer to any location within India as required by the Employer, with 5 days notice and no relocation assistance.

  10. Gratuity

    Gratuity shall be paid at the discretion of the Employer and is conditional upon the Employer receiving payment from its clients.
